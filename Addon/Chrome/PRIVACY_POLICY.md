# Privacy Policy for MorgiFile Chrome Extension

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

MorgiFile ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension interacts with user data.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
MorgiFile is designed as a local-first productivity tool for designers. 
- **Website Content & Web History:** When you explicitly use the "Save to MorgiFile" right-click context menu, the extension temporarily reads the active image URL, page source URL, and page title.
- This metadata is processed solely to transfer the selected visual asset to your own locally hosted companion application (localhost / 127.0.0.1).

## 2. Data Storage and Third Parties
- **Zero Server Storage:** We do not host external servers. All of your saved images, links, and metadata are stored entirely on your local machine via an isolated directory.
- **No Data Sharing or Selling:** We do not track your browsing history, we do not log user activity, and we absolutely NEVER sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties.

## 3. Policy Changes
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the publisher via the developer account email.
